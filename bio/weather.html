Climate of Pinehurst

Pinehurst has is situated in a temperate forest biome with a humid subtropical climate. This combo makes it the ideal location for tourists to immerse themselves in the pure natural environment. Tall trees and friendly forests surround the area with perfect grassy fields for roaming and relaxing in the yearlong sun.

Weather Vibes

Pinehurst's climate offers a delicious warmth throughout the year. The summer seasons especially July are the wettest due to afternoon thunderstorms. Temperatures in the winter months stay relatively around 50 degrees Fahrenheit while maximums reach 80-90 degrees Fahrenheit in the summer months.

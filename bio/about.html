A Resort of Genuinity

The Pinehurst Nature Resort is located in Pinehurst, a small village in the hearts of North Carolina's piedmont region. It houses over three hundred private residential rooms and boastfully staffs over one hundred caterers, servers, waiters and concierge just for you! We are proud to accomodate for the first class visitors whom are looking to escape a dreary world. Visitors are actively encouraged to participate in a range of activities and visit a variety of sights along with tasting the excellent meals from Zesty Seafood Cuisine to Lava Loop Delight only at the Pinehurst Nature Resort.

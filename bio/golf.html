Golfing

Pinehurst is known for its popular leisure activity: golfing. An abundance of fields of various sizes stretch across our front lawn. People of all ages can enjoy participating in this pastime with so many areas to choose from! Golf cart rentals are only $6.99 per hour with golf sticks and balls free with a purchase of a room. Although majority of players will consist of Pinehurst Nature Resort visitors, outsiders can come and savor this recreation.

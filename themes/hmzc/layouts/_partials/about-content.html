My Story

My journey in technology began in third grade, when I first picked up How to Use HTML3 by Scott Arpajian. That early curiosity sparked a lifelong fascination with building—treating code, systems, and infrastructure as puzzle pieces that come together to solve real-world problems. Over the years, I explored everything from designing websites and developing apps to experimenting with low-level software like bootloaders.

Growing up in a quiet suburb of North Carolina, I saw how data influences communities in unique and powerful ways. As data plays an increasingly central role in everyday decision-making, I’ve become motivated to pursue work that helps harness its potential responsibly. After graduating, I plan to build a career in data science or data engineering—fields where I believe thoughtful, well-applied data can empower people and communities everywhere.
